The vault that decides together

A sealed treasury instrument. Graphite surfaces, silver numerals, mono figures on a ruled ledger, and one deliberate blue signal for the things that carry trust: focus, the primary action, and quorum. Anti dark-SaaS: no floating glass, no glow-on-everything, no gradient web3 wordmark. Propose. Approve. Sent.

Surfaces & the one elevation rule

Flat by default: cards separate by border + tone, never by float. Shadow exists at exactly one level — overlays. No hover-lift, no card drop-shadow, no glow.

Delete this vault?

This cannot be undone. The only surface that earns a shadow is a modal.

Voice & anti-cliché

Never

  • A new palette or a new hue — the accent is blue #57a6ff, locked
  • Accent as body text or decoration; it is interactive + quorum only
  • Non-mono money, addresses, txids, or state
  • Floating glass cards, drop-shadow on cards, hover-lift
  • Glow on the mark / seal / lockup; metallic-bevel web3 wordmark
  • Infinite pulse or looping glow
  • Oxblood #7E2A24 / #37493C or purple #863bff literals
  • Crypto jargon (FROST, DKG, nonce), emoji, or em-dashes in copy

Always

  • Mono + right-align on every amount, address, txid, count, date
  • Elevation by border + tone; shadow only on overlays
  • Accent used sparingly and meaningfully
  • Silver as the true secondary metal
  • Success earned (quorum-met / sent / received / live)
  • A visible focus ring; the tarja + nav keyboard-operable
  • Self-hosted fonts, no CDN (local-first)
  • Honest active voice; preview + explicit confirm on money
